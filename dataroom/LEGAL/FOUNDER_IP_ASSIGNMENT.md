## 1. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all right, title, and interest, worldwide, in and to any and all intellectual property, whether now existing or hereafter created, conceived, reduced to practice, or developed by Assignor that relates in any way to:

- The Bickford system, runtime, architecture, and execution governance framework
- Decision continuity, OPTR (Optimal Path Scoring), execution equilibrium, promotion gates, and related concepts
- All software, source code, object code, scripts, models, specifications, documentation, diagrams, and written materials
- All inventions, discoveries, improvements, trade secrets, know-how, and confidential information
- All derivative works and enhancements thereof

## 2. WORKS MADE FOR HIRE; MORAL RIGHTS

To the extent any assigned work does not qualify as a "work made for hire" under applicable law, Assignor hereby assigns all rights therein. Assignor waives any moral rights or similar rights to the fullest extent permitted by law.

## 3. FURTHER ASSURANCES

Assignor agrees to:

(a) Execute any documents and take any actions reasonably requested by Assignee to perfect, enforce, or evidence the assignment;

(b) Cooperate in the prosecution, maintenance, and enforcement of any intellectual property rights;

(c) Provide testimony and evidence in any proceedings related to the assigned intellectual property.

## 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

Assignor represents and warrants that:

(a) Assignor is the sole owner of all assigned intellectual property;

(b) Assignor has full right and authority to make this assignment;

(c) The assigned intellectual property does not infringe any third-party rights;

(d) There are no agreements or obligations that would prevent this assignment;

(e) No prior employer, client, or other party has any claim to the assigned intellectual property.

## 5. POWER OF ATTORNEY

Assignor irrevocably designates and appoints Assignee (and its successors and assigns) as Assignor's attorney-in-fact to execute documents and take actions on Assignor's behalf to perfect the assignment.

## 6. SUCCESSORS AND ASSIGNS

This Assignment binds and benefits the parties and their respective successors and assigns.

## 7. GOVERNING LAW

This Assignment is governed by Delaware law, without regard to conflict of law principles.
