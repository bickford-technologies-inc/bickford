### Board Objection #3 — "Is $25M justified?"

**Response (Sponsor / Corp Dev):**
> "Yes. It's less than the cost of one quarter of delay across multiple senior teams. We're buying speed and control, not growth."

**Why it passes:**
* Boards think in **opportunity cost**, not feature value
* $25M < $17M-$68M build cost (expected value)
* $25M < $1.2B-$3B revenue at risk (if AWS acquires)
* Defensive acquisition, not growth play

**Supporting Data:**
* Internal build: $1.5M-$2.5M direct + $500K-$1M coordination + $5M-$15M failure cost + $10M-$50M opportunity cost = $17M-$68.5M
* Acquisition: $25M total
* **Savings: $0-$43.5M** (expected value)

**Decision:**
✅ **Objection resolved** (price justified by risk avoidance)
