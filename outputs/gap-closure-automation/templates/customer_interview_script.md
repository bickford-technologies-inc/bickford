# GAP-006: Customer Discovery Interview Script

## Introduction

- Briefly introduce Bickford and the purpose of the interview.

## Questions

1. What are your biggest pain points with AI compliance and auditability?
2. How do you currently manage AI decision traceability?
3. What would make you switch to a new compliance platform?
4. What is your budget for compliance solutions?
5. Would you be willing to pilot Bickford?

## Closing

- Thank the participant and ask for permission to follow up.
